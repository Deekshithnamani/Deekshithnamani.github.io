Pattern with Letters

Our brains are wired to recognize or find patterns, logic, and structure even if there is not one. In fact, pattern recognition helps you identify things. You may see with your eyes but all the visual processing is done by the brain, so long story short, you perceive with the brain, this is the foundation of Gestalt principles.

"Patterns with letters" is one such study to understand how people perceive type. This is a fun exploration and experiment of  typefaces.

Let's see if can you identify letters in patterns down below, have fun.
